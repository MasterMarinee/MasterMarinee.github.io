We Are Master Marine

Our company is an Egyptian marine company which was established in 2019 that works in operating, supplying, chartering, forwarding, and providing marine vessels of all kinds. Our company also works in the management of marine projects, contributes to the possibility of providing commercial diving crews, and as a marine broker to provide crews for marine vessels. Master Marine is led by marine experts in various marine fields with more than thirty years of experience. We are always working hard to satisfy and provide marine consultations to our customers, while making sure all of their needs are provided in order to contribute to the advancement of the maritime field as we have always been!
